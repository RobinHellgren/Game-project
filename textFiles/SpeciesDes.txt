First choose your species.

Species:

Human
Humans have moderate agility and strength, but infinite creative capacity, and can learn to use any weapon or charmed item.

Elf
Elves have intense agility and fantastic vision and hearing, but moderate strength and creativity. Some charmed items and weapons are outside of their abilities.

Dwarf
Dwarves are incredibly strong and creative, able to use any weapon. They are slightly slower in combat and occasionally charms refuse to work for them.
